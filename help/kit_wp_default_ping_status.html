Enable trackbacks
Trackbacks are similar to pingbacks, but they're more manual. When you write a blog post and include a link to another blog that has trackbacks enabled, you can send them a trackback notification. This is not done automatically like pingbacks; you usually have to enter the trackback URL provided by the other blog when you publish your post. The difference is that trackbacks can include a title and an excerpt of the content, and they don't require both blogs to be WordPress-based.
